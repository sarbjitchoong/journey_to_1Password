# Story Outline Template

## Chapter 1: Origin

Where the story starts. Mention place, family, values, or early motivation.

## Chapter 2: Study

What you studied and what it taught you.

## Chapter 3: Work

Your chef chapter: discipline, service, pressure, craft, teamwork.

## Chapter 4: Flight

The move from Manila to Montreal. Keep this emotional and visual.

## Chapter 5: Canada

Course, adjustment, learning, and first steps in a new country.

## Chapter 6: Today

Your current company, team, and what you are excited to build next.

## Suggested Speaking Rhythm

- 5 seconds: origin.
- 5 seconds: study.
- 5 seconds: chef work.
- 8 seconds: flight and move.
- 6 seconds: Canada course/job transition.
- 7 seconds: today and gratitude.
